Frequently Asked Questions

How do I capture territory?

Start a session, then walk, run, or cycle in a loop. When your path crosses itself or comes close to where you started, the enclosed area becomes your territory.

Does the app track me when it's closed?

Only during an active session. When you stop your session, all GPS tracking stops. Background tracking only works while a session is running and you've granted "Always Allow" location permission.

Can other players take my territory?

Yes! Other players can capture your territory by walking their own loops through your land. Strengthen your territories by revisiting them to make them harder to capture.

Is Territory Conquest free?

Yes, Territory Conquest is completely free to download and play.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab in the app, then use the search function to find friends by their username. Send them a friend request and once accepted, you'll see each other on the leaderboard.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 February 2026

Territory Conquest Ltd ("we", "our", "us") operates the Territory Conquest mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile picture when you create an account.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS coordinates during active sessions, background location when explicitly enabled during sessions, and route data used to create territory polygons.
  • Activity Data: Distance, duration, speed, steps, routes, and territory data (polygons, areas, strength levels).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, session frequency, and feature usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core gameplay functionality (territory capture, leaderboards, achievements).
  • To display your territories and route on the map.
  • To enable social features (friends, leaderboards, territory battles).
  • To improve the app and fix issues.
  • To send push notifications about territory activity (when enabled by you).

3. Location Data

Location data is central to Territory Conquest's gameplay. We collect GPS data only during active sessions. When you end a session, real-time tracking stops. Background location tracking only operates during active sessions when you have granted "Always Allow" permission, allowing the app to record your route while your screen is locked or you're using other apps.

Your location data is used to create territories, calculate distances, and display your position on the map. Territory polygon data is stored on our servers to enable multiplayer features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your username, territory data, and leaderboard statistics are visible to other players as part of the game. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., database hosting, map services).

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud database) and is protected by industry-standard encryption. Data is stored on servers located in the EU/UK.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Profile > Delete Account). To exercise other rights, contact us at support@territoryconquest.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Territory Conquest is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us
